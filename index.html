Stinger

Stinger is an new app that is going to make the kettle social again!

Busy Office? Quiet Library?

Why not bring people together for a quick tea break? Save friends from disapointing thirst by letting everyone know in advance when the kettle is going to be on!

Manage the kettle simply and efficiently by using one boil for multiple people!

What it can do...

Stinger will change the way you think of the kettle.

Stinger can save water and electricity by sharing the boiling experience between more people.

Multiple people needn't use the kettle in succession as everyone can use it at once!
